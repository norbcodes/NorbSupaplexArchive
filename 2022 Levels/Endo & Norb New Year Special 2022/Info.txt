28.12.2022 - Date of release.

A 20 level long levelset Endo and I made together.
I was originally planning on making my own New Year special, but when I saw Endo was making one too, I just decided to hop in and make some levels for it :)
Every even (2, 4, 6...) level is by me and every odd (1, 3, 5...) is by Endo.
I had lots of fun making these levels, and I hope you'll have as much fun too playing these ;)
Level 14 "Zonkytown" is a level i'm most proud of.
